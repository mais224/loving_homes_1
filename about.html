About Loving Homes

We Care For Dogs Like Family

Loving Homes is a premium dog grooming salon, spa, and dog hotel that provides a safe, clean, and comfortable environment where dogs can relax, play, and enjoy professional care while their owners are away.

Who We Are

A Trusted Place For Every Dog

Our goal is to provide a welcoming and peaceful space for dogs of all sizes and breeds. We offer grooming, spa services, overnight stays, and high-quality daily care in one place.

At Loving Homes, dogs enjoy comfortable rooms with underfloor heating or cooling and television, while owners enjoy peace of mind through pet monitoring, transport services, and 24-hour veterinary support.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality dog care services in a friendly, secure, and professional environment with comfort, safety, and expert support.

Our Vision

To become the preferred dog hotel and grooming destination by offering trusted care, modern facilities, and an outstanding experience for every pet.

Our Values

We value kindness, responsibility, cleanliness, safety, and excellent care in every part of our service.

Our Facilities

Designed For Comfort, Safety, And Fun

Luxury Rooms

Dogs stay in comfortable rooms with underfloor heating or cooling and television.

Safe Outdoor Areas

We provide safe outdoor paddocks, a private grass area, and outdoor play spaces.

Indoor Play Barn

Our indoor play barn allows dogs to stay active and entertained in all weather conditions.

Grooming Salon And Spa

We offer grooming and spa facilities to keep dogs clean, healthy, and relaxed.

Pet Monitoring

Owners can enjoy reassurance through pet monitoring and regular updates.

Extra Support

We offer forest walks, transport to and from the location, and 24-hour veterinary services.

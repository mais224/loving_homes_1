Our Services

Complete Care, Comfort, And Support

At Loving Homes, we provide a complete range of services and facilities including grooming, spa treatments, luxury accommodation, safe outdoor spaces, and extra support for every dog.

Grooming Salon And Spa

We provide grooming and spa services to help dogs stay clean, healthy, relaxed, and comfortable.

Luxury Dog Rooms

Dogs stay in comfortable rooms equipped with underfloor heating or cooling and television.

Transport Service

We offer transport to and from the location for added convenience and easier arrangements for owners.

Safe Outdoor Paddocks

Our outdoor paddocks provide dogs with secure open spaces for exercise, relaxation, and supervised play.

Indoor Play Barn

Dogs can stay active and entertained in our indoor play barn in all weather conditions.

Private Grass Area

We provide a private grass area where dogs can enjoy extra comfort, exercise, and outdoor time.

Forest Walks

Dogs can enjoy forest walks as part of their activity routine in a safe and supervised setting.

Pet Monitoring

We provide pet monitoring and regular updates so owners can feel reassured while their dogs stay with us.

24-Hour Veterinary Support

Veterinary services are available 24 hours a day when required, giving extra safety and reassurance.

Daily Activities

Dogs enjoy daily walks, play sessions, exercise, and relaxing routines designed to keep them active and happy.

General Advice And Support

We are happy to provide general advice about our services and help owners choose the most suitable package.

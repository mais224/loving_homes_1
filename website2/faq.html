FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about our facilities, packages, and services at Loving Homes.

What facilities does Loving Homes provide?

We provide a grooming salon and spa, comfortable rooms with underfloor heating or cooling and television, safe outdoor paddocks, a private grass area, and an indoor play barn.

What packages are available?

We offer Premium, Classic, and Day packages. We also provide custom packages for long stays or dogs with additional requirements.

What is included in the Premium package?

The Premium package includes four walks or activities each day, a private grass area, daily updates and photos, and treats as agreed with the owner.

What is included in the Classic package?

The Classic package includes three walks or activities each day, an indoor play barn, a relaxation area, and a grooming session.

What is included in the Day package?

The Day package includes two walks each day, an outdoor play area, an agility course, and sand digging activities.

Do you offer transport and veterinary support?

Yes, we offer transport to and from the location, and 24-hour veterinary services are available when needed.

How can I arrange a custom package?

Please contact us directly through the Contact page so we can arrange a custom package for long stays or any additional requirements.

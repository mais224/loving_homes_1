Our Packages

Choose The Perfect Package For Your Dog

Loving Homes offers Premium, Classic, and Day packages to suit different needs. Custom packages are also available for long stays or dogs with additional requirements.

Premium Package

Includes four walks or four activities each day, a private grass area, daily updates and photos, and treats as agreed with the owner.

Classic Package

Includes three walks or three activities each day, an indoor play barn, a relaxation area, and a grooming session.

Day Package

Includes two walks each day, an outdoor play area, an agility course, and sand digging activities.

Custom Packages

Available for long stays or dogs with additional requirements. Customers should contact the company to arrange a custom package.

Premium Benefits

Best for dogs that need more activity, private outdoor space, and daily updates for their owners.

Classic Benefits

A balanced package that combines exercise, indoor play, relaxation, and grooming care.

Need Advice?

If you are unsure which package is best for your dog, please contact us for general advice and support.
